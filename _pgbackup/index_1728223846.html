About Geekathon
Geekathon, coding contests for geeks
IT is a step taken by our team to promote programming culture in colleges and help students in grabbing internships and jobs offers from top firms. It is a series of monthly coding challenges to be held on our coding platform practice.geeksforgeeks.org. These challenges will exclusively be designed to help students prepare for the upcoming internships and placement processes at their institutes.
